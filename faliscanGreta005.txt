COMMENTARY
NOTES
@5: Both «Vetter 1953» and «Giacomelli 1963» adopted the suggestion proposed by «Thulin 1907». He suggested that line 1 and line 2 have to be considered as two different inscriptions, referring to two different burials. Thus the tomb was used in different stages for the burial of more than three deceased. To solve the problem of the syntax of this inscription, «Bakkum 2009» suggested that the inscriptions (Bakkum 11 and 12) could be divided into two (respectively ‘cauio pauiceo’ and ‘uolti teti locies cela’), but this suggestion does not consider the epigraphical differences between the two inscriptions
Greta Mozzato
